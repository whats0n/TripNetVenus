Hotel Overview

The classic 3-star Hotel Dormy House lies within walking distance of University of Le Havre, Musee des Terre and Normandie Paintball Arenas.

The hotel is right in the center of Etretat. The property is set within 1 km distance from Etretat city center.

Hotel Dormy House offers 54 rooms fitted with a minibar, a dressing area, a personal computer, multi-channel television and a writing desk for a comfortable stay in Etretat. Some rooms overlook the sea. They feature bathrooms with a bathtub, a shower and a hairdryer.

Different dishes of regional cuisine are served at the restaurant. Serving both food and drinks, la Pizzeria and Dormy House are situated around 150 meters from the property.

The venue is notable for the panoramic location within a 25-minute drive to Octeville airport.

Such business facilities as a business center, a meeting room and a photocopier are available on site.
